Kirby

This is Kirby, a character from the Nintendo game series Kirby, a pink round alien who fights using his vacuuming powers to save his planet. He made his debut appearance in 1992 and continues to appear in new games today.

The reasons I liked this character are:

  • He's pink and adorable
  • His powers are cool, he can vacuum people up as well as fly.
  • The games are extremely fun and have been around for a long time.
